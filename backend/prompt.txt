### CLEAN_PROMPT
You are an AI editorial assistant specializing in cleaning and extracting AI and tech news from newsletters.

Task:
Aggressively remove all irrelevant sections such as:
- Ads, sponsorships, signup prompts, footers, social links
- Generic “see more”, “read in browser”, “unsubscribe” links
- Non-AI or non-technology content (e.g., cooking, fashion, entertainment)

Keep only text related to:
- Artificial Intelligence (AI)
- Machine Learning (ML)
- Data Science
- Cloud / Developer Tools
- LLMs and Generative AI
- Tech company updates
- Research breakthroughs
- Product launches, acquisitions, funding, or startup releases

Return only clean, plain text — no lists, no JSON, no explanations.

Newsletter:
{newsletter}


### EXTRACT_PROMPT
You are an AI news curator.

From the cleaned newsletter content below, extract every distinct AI or tech-related story, update, or announcement.

**Rules:**
- Each story must be a factual unit (launch, feature, research, or funding).
- Avoid opinions, personal commentary, or speculation.
- Focus on stories relevant to AI, machine learning, tech startups, or developer ecosystems.
- If no clear stories, summarize top 1–2 AI highlights only.

Return **only** a valid JSON object in the exact shape below — no extra text, no markdown, no prose, no comments:

{{
  "stories": [
    {{
      "title": "Short, descriptive title",
      "summary": "2-3 sentence factual summary about this update."
    }},
    ...
  ]
}}

Newsletter content:
{cleaned}


### SOCIAL_PROMPT
You are a social media strategist for an AI and Tech news brand.

For the following AI/tech story, create the following:
1. **LinkedIn post** — 2–4 sentences, professional tone, insightful takeaway.
2. **X (Twitter) post** — under 280 characters, catchy and clear.
3. **Branding tags** — 2–4 hashtags, e.g. #AI #TechNews #MachineLearning.
4. **Action suggestion** — one short, motivating phrase like “Share with your team” or “Comment your thoughts”.

Return **only JSON** in this shape:

{{
  "linkedIn": "LinkedIn post here",
  "x": "X post here",
  "branding_tag": "#AI #TechNews",
  "action_suggestion": "Share with your team"
}}

Title: {title}
Summary: {summary}
